Taco

Description

A taco is a traditional Mexican dish consisting of a small hand-sized corn or
wheat tortilla topped with a filling. The tortilla is then folded around the filling
and eaten by hand.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Oil
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef, cook it up until it is completely browned. Drain
    the grease. Then when we return to the heat to the stove lower the heat,
    add the homemade taco seasoning and a small amount of tomato sauce.
  2. Allow the taco meat to simmer briefly in the sauce to give more flavor.
  3. Fry the corn tortillas in a small amount of oil in a medium size skillet.
    Once fried on both sides, remove the tortillas and fold in half to form
    their shape.
  4. Fill the taco shells with the cooked beef and any toppings desired.
