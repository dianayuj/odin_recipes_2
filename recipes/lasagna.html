Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the
type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers
of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese,
in between the lasagna noodles.

Overview of layers

  • Cheese Filling: Mix the ricotta and cheeses (per recipe below) with an egg in a bowl,
    and set aside. No ricotta? No problem, cottage cheese works just fine in this recipe!
  • Meat Sauce:Brown the onion, garlic, and meat in a pot on the stovetop. Add the pasta sauce
    and tomato paste & simmer a few minutes
  • Layer Together:Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles
    and bake until bubbling

How to Layer Lasagna

  1. Spread about a cup of meat sauce into a 9x13 pan. Add a layer of noodles.
  2. Top the noodles with some of the cheese mixture.
  3. Repeat the layers, ending with a layer of noodles and sauce
  4. Cover with foil and bake.
  5. Remove foil, top with mozzarella and parmesan and bake another 15 minutes.
